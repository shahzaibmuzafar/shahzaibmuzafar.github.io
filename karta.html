Setup Instructions

To set up our versatile Discord music bot, use the command /setup [channel_name] in any voice or text channel. This command creates a controller for seamless management.

  • Easily configure the bot with a simple setup command.
  • Manage settings in any Discord channel.
  • Keep your controller channel organized and clutter-free.

Enjoy the simplicity and efficiency of our setup process. It's designed to enhance your Discord experience effortlessly.
